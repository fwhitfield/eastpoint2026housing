The Plan (Executive Summary)

Purpose. The 2026 Housing Plan focuses City capacity on five priorities that move residents from vulnerability to stability while laying the groundwork for long-term affordability.

How we’ll deliver. A disciplined annual calendar, clear governance through GICH, quarterly Council updates, and a living data dashboard—sequenced to protect staff capacity while maintaining momentum.

Outcome. By the end of 2026: more residents prepared to buy homes, more homes preserved, a stronger affordable housing pipeline, policy tools to safeguard affordability, and tighter internal alignment between housing and homelessness prevention—reducing the number of households that fall into crisis.
